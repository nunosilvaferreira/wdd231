Community Serve Hub - Site Plan

1. Site Name

Community Serve Hub

This name was selected because it clearly communicates the purpose of the site - to serve as a central hub connecting volunteers with community service opportunities. The name is straightforward, memorable, and describes exactly what the site offers.

Optional domain availability: communityservehub.org

2. Site Purpose

The Community Serve Hub website will provide:

  • A centralized platform for local nonprofit organizations to post volunteer opportunities
  • Tools for volunteers to search and filter opportunities by location, cause, and date
  • Information about community impact and success stories
  • Resources for both volunteers and organizations to maximize their service efforts
  • A registration system for volunteers to track their service hours

3. Scenarios

These questions represent what our target audience might ask:

  1. "Where can I find volunteer opportunities near me this weekend?" - This drives our search and filtering functionality.
  2. "How can my nonprofit organization post our volunteer needs on this platform?" - This informs our organization registration and posting system.
  3. "What kind of impact have volunteers made through this platform?" - This leads to our impact statistics and success stories section.

  • Secondary (#F4A261): Secondary buttons, highlights
  • Accent (#E9C46A): Hover states, small interactive elements
  • Text (#333333): Body text
  • Background (#F8F9FA): Page background

5. Typography

Font Selections:

  • Montserrat (Sans-serif): Used for all headings (h1, h2, h3)
  • Open Sans (Sans-serif): Used for body text and all other content

These fonts were chosen for their readability, modern appearance, and complementary pairing. Both are widely available through Google Fonts.
